Objectives:

  • understand what I read, see, & hear about volunteering in my community and other communities in the Spanish-speaking world
  • exchange opinions related to volunteering in other countries and provide reasons
  • can participate in a conversation with someone about ways to serve their community & its benefits.
  • exchange information to make plans to participate in local volunteer opportunities with a group of friends.
  • exchange e-mail to discuss volunteer opportunities
  • talk about a volunteering experience related to my passions & interests.
  • give a presentation about the volunteering/ community service activities or someone else, such as a famous athlete, celebrity, or community figure.
  • I can write a comparison of volunteering in communities to determine what is valued in my own and other cultures.
  • I can talk about my day and describe how I can help my community
